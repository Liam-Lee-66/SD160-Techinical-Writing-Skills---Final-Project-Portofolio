Lightsaber Duelling Guide

Author: Liam Lee

Published: 12/08/2021

Introduction

Star Wars: Battlefront 2, is a well-known game to many Star Wars fans, thanks to its many popular game modes. One of the most popular game modes is Heroes vs Villains. Heroes vs Villains is a 4 vs 4 or a 2 vs 2 duels between Heroes and Villains. In this game mode, you can select a hero or a villain that has appeared in the Star Wars films, and fight your opponents.

The game mode Heroes vs Villains can be overwhelming for new players, due to their low level and experience. This guide will be helping out the new players how to level up their character efficiently, and how to duel their opponents to their advantage.

Heads Up!

This guide is based on what I have learned throughout my experience playing the game. There can be some information missing or added compared to other guides.

This guide does not go over the basic controls of the video game. I recommend trying the tutorial first and then going over this guide.

Strengthening Characters

Star Cards

Levelling up characters is one of the most important things to do if you wish to get stronger. When players level up, they receive ‘points’ which can be used to buy or upgrade Star Cards. These Star Cards are used to upgrade the character’s abilities. Meaning, the higher level, the stronger your character is. Thanks to these ability upgrades, you can have an advantage in defeating your opponent.

Star Card upgrades can include:

  • Increase in maximum health
  • Increase in normal attack damage, but with more cost on Stamina
  • Increased range in abilities
  • Increased damage in abilities
  • Decreased ability cool downtime
  • Decrease incoming damage
  • Etc

Levelling Up

There are many different ways to level up your characters. However, many of those ways are not very efficient. The most efficient way of upgrading any character is by playing Co-op.

The game mode Co-op is where 4 players team up and try to defeat the AI enemies. Thanks to the AI’s low difficulty, new players can easily get used to their characters and earn experience points.

There are many different battle situations in Co-op, preventing the players to get bored while playing. Players can also select any era they wish to fight in. They can fight during the Clone Wars, the Galactic Civil War, or during the sequel trilogy.

Lightsaber Duelling

One of the most common mistakes that new players make while lightsaber duelling is spamming melee attacks. Similar to real-life duelling, this will leave the character vulnerable to the opponent’s attacks. This can be because of you wasting your stamina, or the opponent is parrying your attacks.

Timing is the most important when duelling. You must control when to strike and when to not. Not only this will help you to control the usage of your stamina, but this will also prevent you from rushing into battle without any second thoughts.

Stamina Control

Stamina will be used when attacking and defending. For some characters, stamina can be also used for using the character’s ability. When the character’s stamina is depleted, the character is no longer able to attack or defend. Stamina will recharge when not attacking or defending. The recharging will stop when you start attacking or defending.

You can also see how much stamina your opponents have. This will come in very handy since you can compare your stamina and the opponents. This could help you make decisions as to whether to attack your opponent or fall back.

Parrying

Parrying is a difficult skill to master, but it is the most useful skill to have. Parrying is when a player blocks an incoming attack and strikes back immediately after. Every character will have a short second where they are vulnerable to attacks right after when they strike. The split second of vulnerability is what you will be needing to aim for when parrying.

To parry, you will be needing to block the first attack. Blocking the first attack will make the opponent locate themselves into your striking zone. While blocking, when you hear the lightsabers clashing, you will need to strike (if you wish to). Depending on the character’s striking speed, you will be needing to adjust when to strike. However, the sound of the lightsabers clashing should be the benchmark for you to when to strike.

Parrying is only useful when you have only one opponent to focus your defence on. Meaning, when you are surrounded by several opponents, parrying won’t be the greatest option to use.

Another downside of parrying is related to the usage of your stamina. As said above, you will be needing to block the opponent’s attack and then strike immediately. Obviously, this will cost a lot of stamina because you are blocking an attack and then striking. This is likely to repeat, so you won’t be able to recharge unless you start dodging. Make sure to be aware of your stamina at all times.

Summary

Remember to always think about which action to make rather than striking without any other thoughts. This will prevent you from making any unnecessary risks during the fight.

Always be aware of your surroundings. This includes your stamina, the opponent’s stamina, and the opponent’s position. Stamina control is crucial to attack and defend. Compare your stamina and your opponent’s to make decisions as to whether to strike the opponent or back away. Make sure to strike, when the opponent is in range. If not this will leave you vulnerable, and give the opponent the advantage.

Most importantly, never, ever spam your attacks. This can cost your stamina, but most importantly, your opponent can easily parry you.

Good luck, or in other words, May the force be with you!
